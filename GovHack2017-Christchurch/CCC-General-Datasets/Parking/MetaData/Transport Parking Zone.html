        Title: Transport Parking Zone
    Description:
      Abstract:
        Parking Zone describes a length of kerbing and whether that length is available or restricted for vehicle parking. Where a Resolution Order revokes a Parking zone, the current record should be made Obsolete. A Proposed Parking zone should change to In Service when a Resolution Order to implement is Approved. It is abstracted to a Line and its vertices are coincident with a ParkingKerb.
        Entity Type: Concept
      Purpose: A spatial representation of available or restricted parking zones along a length of kerbing.
      Supplemental Information: Maintained and available in SQL Server.
